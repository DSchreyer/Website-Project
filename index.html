About Me!

I am a Data Scientist and Bioinformatician working at the intersection of oncology, pharmaceutical R&D, and clinical data analytics. My focus is translating high-dimensional biological and trial-related datasets into clear, decision-ready evidence.

I design and maintain reproducible analysis pipelines (including Nextflow-based workflows) for NGS and multi-omics data, with emphasis on robustness, traceability, and scalability for regulated and collaborative environments.

In my current pharma-facing work, I support oncology and clinical-trial contexts through pipeline development, data integration, statistical analysis, and communication of results to cross-functional teams.
