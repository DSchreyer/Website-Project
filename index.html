About Me!

I finished my M.Sc. in Cell and Molecular Biology at the Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU).

During my master's I gained deep insights into the field of bioinformatics and wrote my master's thesis about a scRNA-seq processing pipeline, and the analysis of scRNA-seq data in the group of Prof. Dr. Leila Taher, FAU.

I am looking forward to a Ph.D. position in the area of bioinformatics, to apply and extend my current knowledge.

SCPP: a single-cell RNA-seq processing pipeline

In my master's thesis, I worked on the construction of a pipeline to process single-cell RNA-seq data that allows the user to perform individual downstream analysis (GitHub Repository). Additionally, I performed the downstream analysis on data which was generated by Johann Helmut Brandstätter's group (FAU). The aim of the project was to identify the molecular mechanisms underlying the phenotypic differences observed between two murine Bsn mutants.
