About Me!

I finished my M.Sc. in Cell and Molecular Biology at the Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU).

During my master's I gained deep insights into the field of bioinformatics and wrote my master's thesis about a scRNA-seq processing pipeline, and the analysis of scRNA-seq data in the group of Prof. Dr. Leila Taher, FAU.

I am looking forward to a Ph.D. position in the area of bioinformatics, to apply and extend my current knowledge.
